About Me

I am a UX designer with an organizational and marketing background based in the Munich area, passionate about creating products that are more accessible and enjoyable to use. In my past work experiences, I’ve learned how to translate ideas into results by researching, outlining concepts, and implementing them. A structured approach has always helped me to stay on top of things. My knowledge about Scrum gives me an understanding of agile and user-centered thinking. I also like good design and pixel-perfection, paying a close attention to details. During the Certified UX Design program from Careerfoundry, I developed two responsive web applications covering user research, user journeys, information architecture, wireframing, prototyping, and UI design, using prototyping- and design tools such as Balsamiq, InVision, and Sketch. I realized that a user-centered approach is a key element in creating a successful solution. Now I want to apply my problem solving skills to UX design. I am currently looking for full-time opportunities as UX designer in the Munich area. Like what you see? Let’s connect!
